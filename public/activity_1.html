IOT APPS

Through this task, what did I understood about the an IOT application ?

IOT application is a platform which connects various devices to the internet, so that they can interact with each other, i.e., analyze the data.

What are the main features needed in it?

  • Connectivity
  • Intelligence
  • Energy
  • Safety
  • Interaction with internet
  • Artificial Intelligence

What are some examples?

  1. Smart Surveillance
  2. Air Quality Management
  3. Wearable Devices
  4. Smart Home Security Systems
