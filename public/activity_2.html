Smart Building

What does smart mean?

By the word smart, we mean that the automated device can respond and interact digitally.
It it able to solve a complex problem in a simpler way and operate autonomously to some extent.

What are the main features needed?

  • Internet Access
  • Building Automation
  • Life Safety
  • Telecommunication
  • Sensors to help initiate actuators
  • Reduced energy consumption

What are some smart-building scenarios?

  1. Temperature/Climate controlled buildings
  2. Smart TVs connection to the internet to access content through applications.
  3. Some smart TVs also include voice or gesture recognition.
  4. High-level Security,
  5. Using smart locks and garage-door openers, users can grant or deny access to visitors. Installing Solar panels
